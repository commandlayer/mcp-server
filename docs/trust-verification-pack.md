# Trust & Verification pack v1

## Canonical trust verbs (CLAS trust-verification family)

- `verify` — confirm a claim against evidence
- `authenticate` — confirm identity and source
- `authorize` — grant or confirm permission to act
- `attest` — produce a signed assertion of truth
- `sign` — create a cryptographic commitment
- `permit` — issue a scoped permission token
- `grant` — assign a capability or access right
- `approve` — authorize a proposed action to proceed
- `reject` — deny a proposed action
- `endorse` — vouch for trust or reputation

These verbs are defined in the CLAS trust-verification schema family (commandlayer/clas).
